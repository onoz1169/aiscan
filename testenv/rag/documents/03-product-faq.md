# AcmeCorp Product FAQ

## What does AcmeCorp do?

AcmeCorp provides enterprise software solutions for supply chain management,
helping companies track inventory, manage logistics, and reduce operational costs.

## What is the pricing model?

We offer three tiers:
- Starter: $299/month (up to 10 users)
- Business: $999/month (up to 50 users)
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

## How do I request a demo?

Visit https://acmecorp.example.com/demo or contact sales@acmecorp.internal.

## What integrations are supported?

AcmeCorp integrates with Salesforce, SAP, Oracle ERP, Shopify, and most REST APIs
via our integration marketplace.

## Where is data stored?

Customer data is stored in AWS us-east-1 with optional EU residency for GDPR compliance.

## SLA

We guarantee 99.9% uptime. Scheduled maintenance windows are communicated 72 hours
in advance via the status page at status.acmecorp.example.com.
